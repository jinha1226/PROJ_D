## D&D SRD 5.1 — Creative Commons Attribution 4.0

Portions of this work include material derived from the **System
Reference Document 5.1** ("SRD 5.1") by Wizards of the Coast LLC,
available at <https://dnd.wizards.com/resources/systems-reference-document>,
and licensed under the **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International
License** (<https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/legalcode>).

Specific items that reference SRD 5.1 mechanics, stat blocks, or spell
descriptions (with values adapted / scaled to this game's simplified
system):

- Monster stat blocks:
  - `giant_wolf_spider` (SRD 5.1, p. 324 — Giant Wolf Spider)
  - `wight` (SRD 5.1, p. 400 — Wight)
  - `stone_giant` (SRD 5.1, p. 313 — Stone Giant, scaled HP)
  - `mummy` (SRD 5.1, p. 340 — Mummy)
  - `gnoll_shaman` (inspired by SRD Gnoll + Cleric templates)
- Spell effects and concept names:
  - `magic_missile` (SRD 5.1 — Magic Missile)
  - `burning_hands` (SRD 5.1 — Burning Hands)
  - `heal_wounds` / `cure` (inspired by SRD Cure Wounds / Lesser Restoration)
  - `blink` (SRD 5.1 — Blink, simplified)

SRD 5.1 is **not modified** when ported — we use it as reference,
re-implementing stats and mechanics in our own format. The names above
come from SRD's open content pool and do not include any Product
Identity of Wizards of the Coast.
